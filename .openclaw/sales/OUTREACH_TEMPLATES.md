## EMAIL BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
- ‚úÖ Personalize first line (reference recent news, LinkedIn activity, or mutual connection)
- ‚úÖ Use bullet points and short paragraphs (mobile-friendly)
- ‚úÖ Include ONE clear CTA (calendar link, question, or reply instruction)
- ‚úÖ Add social proof (customer name, industry, or metric)
- ‚úÖ Attach value (one-pager, demo video, ROI calculator)

### DON'T:
- ‚ùå Send generic "spray and pray" emails
- ‚ùå Use more than 2-3 colors/fonts (looks like spam)
- ‚ùå Ask for >30 minutes in first outreach (too much commitment)
- ‚ùå Name-drop customers without permission (check contracts for NDA clauses)
- ‚ùå Follow up more than 4 times without a reply (you're now spam)
